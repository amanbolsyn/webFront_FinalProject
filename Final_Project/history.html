2015
Website Launch
Our recipe website was launched with the goal of helping people discover new recipes and share their culinary creations with others.
2016
Added Recipe Categories
We introduced various recipe categories like "Breakfast", "Lunch", "Dinner", and "Desserts" to help users easily find recipes that suit their tastes.
2017
User Profiles
A new feature was launched allowing users to create profiles where they could save their favorite recipes and share their own creations with the community.
2018
Recipe Sharing & Social Media Integration
We integrated social media features, allowing users to easily share recipes on platforms like Facebook and Instagram directly from the website.
2019
Mobile App Launch
Our mobile app was launched to allow users to access their favorite recipes, shopping lists, and meal plans on the go.
2020
Advanced Search Filters
To help users find exactly what they’re looking for, we introduced advanced search filters including dietary preferences (e.g., vegan, gluten-free) and cooking time.
2021
User Recipe Reviews
We allowed users to leave reviews and ratings on recipes to help others find the best dishes and provide feedback to the recipe creators.
2022
International Cuisine Added
We expanded our recipe database to include international cuisines, making it easier for users to explore dishes from around the world.
2023
Shopping List Feature
We launched a shopping list feature, allowing users to automatically generate grocery shopping lists based on the ingredients of their chosen recipes.
